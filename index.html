Why Join Us?

Certification

Add value to you CV with and SSUIMUN certificate which speaks to your excellent communication skills, knowledge of current affairs and much more

Improved Soft Skills

SSUIMUN events offer valuable opportunities to enhance essential soft skills like public speaking, critical thinking, problem-solving, teamwork, and negotiation. Participants also gain increased global awareness. These skills are highly prized by future employers across all fields

Big Cash Prize

The top-performing delegates at SSUIMUN will have the chance to win massive cash prizes. Along with the cash prizes, participants and winners are awarded with SSUIMUN goodies and sale vouchers from top brands

Great Socials and Hospitality

You get to experience vibrant social nights, and the chance to forge global friendships. SSUIMUN is renowned for its exceptional hospitality in the region. Enjoy comfortable accommodations and meals provided by us.

Experienced Executive Board Members

SSUIMUN events are led by exceptional executive board members highly experienced in chairing MUNs professionally. These individuals will be there to support and moderate the discussion throughout the event and to help the delegates have a positive and rewarding experience.
